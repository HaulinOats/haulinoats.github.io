What is Financial Forest?

Financial Forest turns goals into habits. Enter a longterm savings goal and we'll tell you what to do on a weekly basis. Your tree grows as you save and meet your goals.

How it Helps You

Financial Forest makes saving easy. There are no spreadsheets, budgets or math. Just simple guidance to get you from this week to next week.We'll tell you what to deposit in your savings account and when. We'll also help you get through the week with motivational tips.

How to Get Started

  • It's as easy as 1-2-3.
  • 1. Enter a savings goal
  • 2. Enter a target date
  • 3. Make your weekly savings deposit

After that, you'll receive push notifications to help keep you motivated and on track. Get started and grow with us!

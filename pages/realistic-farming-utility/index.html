Stardew Valley - Realistic Farming Utility

Are you tired of the farming aspect of Stardew Valley being too predictable, knowing you can just resort to posts and articles online that tell you the objectively best crops to maximize profit? This utility will pseudo-randomize all the properties of both seeds and crops to help keep farming balanced but also, randomize crops and seeds values so you'll have to figure out the most efficient crops on your own.

This utility it built to work with the Stardew Valley mod Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming courtesy of o0MrPro.

How to use

Upload your the content.json file that exists inside the [CP] Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming mod folder and then click Generate. The file will be updated with new psuedo-randomized crop values (harvest time, sell prices, regrowth capability, etc) so you'll have to figure out the most efficient crops on your own. Then simply place this file back into the [CP] Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming mod folder, overwriting the existing content.json file. *Please ensure you're placing it in the [CP] Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming mod folder and NOT the [STF] Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming mod folder (which handles store-related modifications*

Stardew Valley - Realistic Farming Utility

Are you tired of the farming aspect of Stardew Valley being too predictable, knowing you can just resort to posts and articles online that tell you the objectively best crops to maximize profit? This utility will pseudo-randomize all the properties of both seeds and crops to help keep farming balanced but also, randomize crops and seeds values so you'll have to figure out the most efficient crops on your own.

This utility it built to work with the Stardew Valley mod Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming courtesy of o0MrPro.

To view calculations and data output, open the developer console (right click anywhere on the page and select "inspect" then click on "console" panel). You can also reference this link if you're having trouble figuring it out.

How to use

Upload the content.json file that exists inside the [CP] Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming mod folder and then click the Generate content.json file button. The file will be updated with new psuedo-randomized crop values (harvest time, sell prices, regrowth capability, etc). Simply place this file back into the [CP] Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming mod folder, overwriting the existing content.json file.

** Please ensure you place it in the [CP] Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming mod folder and NOT the [STF] Balanced Valley - Realistic Farming mod folder (which handles store-related modifications) **

Modifiers

Total Regrowth Crops Multiplier

Percentage of how many crops per season are allowed to have regrowth capability

Allows for whole or floating points (ex. 15, 12.5, 0.5, etc)

Short Crop Maturation Range

Range of days for "short-term" crops to mature (min 5 days)

Whole numbers only (ex. 4, 5, 8, etc)

Medium Crop Maturation Range

Range of days for "medium-term" crops to mature

Whole numbers only (ex. 9, 12, 15, etc)

Long Crop Maturation Range

Range of days for "long-term" crops to mature (max 112 days)

Whole numbers only (ex. 16, 23, 92, etc)

Regular Seed Gold Per Day Purchase Price Multiplier Range

Gold per day range multiplier for seed purchase price

Whole numbers only (ex. 4, 5, 8, etc)

Regular Crop Gold Per Day Sell Price Multiplier Range

Gold per day range multiplier for crop sell price

Whole numbers only (ex. 4, 5, 8, etc)

Regrowth Seed Purchase Price Multiplier Range

Gold per day range multiplier for regrowth seed purchase price

Whole numbers only (ex. 4, 5, 8, etc)

Regrowth Crop Gold Per Day Sell Price Multiplier Range

Gold per day range multiplier for regrowth crop sell price

Whole numbers only (ex. 4, 5, 8, etc)

3 following inputs should add up to 100

Total Short Crops Per Season Multiplier

Percentage of how many crops per season will fall within the "short" maturation time (4 - 8 days)

Allows for whole or floating points (ex. 5, 12, 15.5, etc)

Total Medium Crops Per Season Multiplier

Percentage of how many crops per season will fall within the "medium" maturation time (9 - 15 days)

Allows for whole or floating points (ex. 5, 12, 15.5, etc)

Total Long Crops Per Season Multiplier

Percentage of how many crops per season will fall within the "long" maturation time (16 - 112 days)

Allows for whole or floating points (ex. 5, 12, 15.5, etc)
